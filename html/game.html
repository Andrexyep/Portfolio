My Game Design Works

BroccoMeat

BroccoMeat is a game developed during the Global Game jam in only 10 days!
This is a Shooter Party Game where two factions Vegans and Carnivores have to fight till the end of a timer, who conquer the most win!

Event: Jam

Role: Game&Level Designer

SINK

SINK was developed during the Zagarolo Game Jam by a team o four people.
Japan is about to be submerged by the sea. Play in VR a scientist who is trying to save Japan by developing a new technology while his country, family and memories is sinking.

Event: Jam

Role: Game&Level Designer

Bathtub of Dune

This game was developed in Aiv by a team of four Game Designers as a commission from out teachers.
You have to find the 3 king heads in order to access the bathtub but be careful, during the day the desert is hot and you will cook alive if you stay too long in the sun.

Event: School Project

Role: Game&Level Designer

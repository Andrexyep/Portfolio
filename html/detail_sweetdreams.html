My Role

In this Project I worked as a Level Designer, I made several levels and blockout for my team. Following my Lead Game Designer instruction I tried to give meaning in every level I made telling the story of the place where the protagonist is with different elements of enrichment and player interactions.

Our Team!

How we Started

For the first day, our Game Designer team, with the help of our Lead Programmer Andrea and Lead Artist Miriam, created a ten days pipeline for each of us smaller teams to follow. For this 10 days we have decided to leave the last 2 days as a pillow time, to refine the game and fix it where it is needed. Most of our work as Game Designers was done during the first two days, to allow other teams to work as soon as possible as fast as possible, without any worries.

Documentation

The first document we have done was a high vision, That's because we wanted to make sure our team understand the concept of the game and gameplay that we as Game Designers aimed. This also helped us to design and polish the mechanics we wanted to highlight and the feelings we want to convey to our player.

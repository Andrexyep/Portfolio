Analyze the AI and Combact Trigger

I decided to start doing some research on differents survival games to analyze the core combact systems, the encounter and combact trigger as well as the anticipation and the end of the trigger condition.

  • Rule-based AI: An AI programmed to follow a simple set of rules.
  • Finite State Machines AI: An AI that can move between several states, each state as a different purpose.
  • Adaptive AI: An AI that evaluate the game context and decide based on that.

Conclusion:

  • Most of the games have "scripted encounter" or ruled by other factor of the game. Usually the trigger that initiates the Combact System is an overlapped of the Player and the Vision Cone of the entity concerned.
  • Most of the games have a "Finite State Machines AI". This because this type of AI have a more rational combact system design or "humanoid".

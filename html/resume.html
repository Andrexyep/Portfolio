Professional Info

I'm a designer who loves his craft more than everything! My specialty is Level design, but I'm also versatile with good programming skills and a deep understanding of game design.

As a designer, I seek to create emotional and unforgettable gameplay moments.

I specialize in designing levels for single-player games where narration is married to gameplay to provide a deep emotional experience.

I always seek to challenge my creativity to create ever new experiences that try to surprise and impress the players.

I strongly believe that video games are a very powerful medium to convey and create immersive experiences that touch the deepest emotions of the players.

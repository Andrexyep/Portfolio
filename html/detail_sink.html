My Role

Sink was developed in 7 days with a team of one Programmer, one Game Designer and two 3D Artist. In such a short time my main role was to work on the level design and some easy 3D assets. Our goal for this Jam was to develop an interactive VR experience through the memories of our protagonist who along the way will rediscover his hometown now submerged by water.
